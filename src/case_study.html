Socotra
Prototype Socotra:
The Island of Dreams

As stated in other pages, I developed a prototype about the island of Socotra to showcase its beauty in all categories: Flora, Fauna, and People. Currently, I'm planning on re-working on it in order for it to officially be the first project to be included in my portfolio.

Although it's not visible here, the original prototype that I designed does look childish and unprofessional. However, despite those flaws, that prototype was the first one I committed myself in doing and which I'm still proud.

The reason why I believe it is the strongest of the projects is because I managed to complete it and used all my expertise to not only help pass the WEB 2500 class but to also give me a taste of what my future career is going to be for my following years.
